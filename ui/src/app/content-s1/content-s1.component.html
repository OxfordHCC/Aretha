Introduction to the Privacy Assistant

The privacy assistant helps you understand how data is flowing in and out of your home

  • Data flows from your devices whenever you do things like use an app on your smartphone, load a website on your computer, upload activities from your Fitbit, or remotely switch on the heating.
  • Connect devices to the privacy assistant's WiFi hotspot to start investigating your data streams.
  • You can review data flows as they happen by using the time series tab, or look at your overall data exposure to different companies using the overview tab.

The privacy assistant helps you understand how data is an integral part of smart home devices.

  • All of your devices send and receive data when you use them actively.
  • Devices might also send and receive data when they are 'waiting'.
  • Manufacturers use data to provide features, monitor the device status, and for many more things.
